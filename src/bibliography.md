## Bibliography & Further Reading

We consulted the following sources (to various degrees) while creating and interpreting visualizations for this project, especially in looking for causal factors to explain the patterns we observed:

**World Bank – World Development Indicators (Primary Dataset).**  
https://data.worldbank.org/indicator  

**World Bank – Country Income Classifications.**  
https://datahelpdesk.worldbank.org/knowledgebase/articles/906519  

**UNDP Human Development Report – Life Expectancy, Education, and Inequality Data.**  
https://hdr.undp.org/  


**IPCC Sixth Assessment Report – Global Carbon Trends and Mitigation.**  
https://www.ipcc.ch/assessment-report/ar6/  

**Easterlin, Richard. “Does Economic Growth Improve the Human Lot?”**  
Foundational research on the link between income and well-being.

**Pritchett, L. & Summers, L. “Wealthier is Healthier.”**  
Examines how higher income levels correlate with improved social outcomes.

**UNESCO Institute for Statistics – Literacy Indicators.**  
http://uis.unesco.org  

**Observable Framework Documentation – Project Architecture.**  
https://observablehq.com/framework  

**D3.js API Reference – Visualization Library Used Throughout the Project.**  
https://github.com/d3/d3/blob/master/API.md
